Activities for Infants

Tummy Time

Description: Tummy time is a delightful activity that encourages infants to spend time on their stomachs while awake. This position helps strengthen their neck, shoulder, and back muscles, which are essential for future milestones like rolling over, sitting up, and crawling. You can make tummy time more engaging by placing colorful toys just out of reach or lying down next to your baby to encourage interaction.

Cost: Free

Special Clothing: Comfortable onesie

Duration: 10-15 minutes

Local Communities: Parent support groups often organize sessions to share tips on tummy time.

Health Considerations: Always supervise the infant during tummy time to ensure their safety.

History: Pediatricians have recommended tummy time since the 1990s as a preventive measure against flat head syndrome, emphasizing its importance in developing motor skills.
