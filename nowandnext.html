How To

Effectively manage your tasks with these simple steps:

  • View Tasks: Click on a category button (e.g., Chores, Crafting) to see a list of available tasks.
  • Add Tasks: Click on a task to add it to the "Now" area.
  • Manage Tasks: Click on a task in the "Now" or "Next" area to move it or delete it.
  • Drag and Drop: Move tasks between "Now" and "Next" areas as needed.

Remember, you can only have a maximum of 5 tasks in each area.
